Effie's Top 3 Movies

My 3 favorite movies of all time

Mamma Mia

This movie is a great pick me up. Its so fun to watch and sing along with since it is a musical. Its light hearted and hilarious.

Girl Interrupted

I love this movie. Its an emotional and hard to watch story but crucial as it looks into more of the harder parts of growing up and being an adult.

Burlesque

This movie is awesome and musically entertaining. It reminds me of why I love Christina Aguilera & Cher so much.
